Econoveyor
Description:The Econoveyor is designed to move parts from the discharge of a machine directly into a bin, relieving operators from repetitive unloading tasks and enhancing workflow efficiency. This lightweight, self-lubricating conveyor system eliminates the need for an expensive chain, opting instead for a sprocket-driven hinged belt. Built with minimal welding, its bolt-together design further reduces manufacturing costs, making it a cost-effective solution to compete with overseas manufacturers.
